The beginning

Built on the idea
that less is more.

Rhode was born from a personal frustration with the beauty industry — too many products, too many promises, and not enough truth. We started by asking a simple question: what does skin actually need?

The answer became our obsession. Clean, clinically studied formulas that work with your skin instead of on top of it. No fillers. No excess. Just exactly what earns its place.

what's inside

Conscientious ingredients.

Hyaluronic Acid

Sodium Hyaluronate

A hydration powerhouse that draws moisture into the skin and holds up to 1,000× its weight in water. We use multi-weight HA for deep and surface hydration simultaneously.

Peptide Complex

Palmitoyl Oligopeptide

Signal peptides communicate with skin cells to stimulate collagen production, improving firmness and elasticity over time. The cornerstone of our Lip Tint formula.

Niacinamide

Vitamin B3

A multitasking vitamin that minimises pores, fades discolouration, and reinforces the skin barrier. Gentle enough for all skin types, effective enough to show real results.
